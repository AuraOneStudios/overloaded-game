### Tagline (Short description)
> A top-down roguelite horde survivor where you play as an engineer wolf. Survive until you can summon Archie, a giant combat robot, and become OVERPOWERED!

### Description (Long description)
> In a chaotic world where gears and oil dictate the rules of survival, meet **Lizzie**, an extremely skilled, fearless engineer and mechanic wolf. She never goes into a fight unarmed, using her inseparable Wrench Boomerang to keep enemies at a distance.
> 
> But Lizzie's true power is revealed in the critical moment. When enemy batteries are drained and she reaches the limit of exhaustion, her ultimate invention comes into play: **Archie**, a giant combat robot.
> 
> Riding Archie, Lizzie literally becomes **OVERPOWERED**, slaughtering entire hordes with plasma cannons and brutal trampling!
> 
> ---
> 
> ### 🎮 Gameplay Features
> - **Fluid & Frantic Core Loop**: 8-direction isometric movement focusing on dodging, grouping enemies, and tactical positioning.
> - **Overload Mode**: Survive to fill your energy bar and unleash Archie! The character's controls, attacks, and visuals change instantly, granting you devastating new abilities (Plasma Cannon & Trample).
> - **Roguelite Horde Survival**: Test your skills and see how long you can survive the endless waves of robotic and mechanical horrors.
